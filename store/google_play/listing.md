# WashDesk Google Play Listing

## App Name

WashDesk

## Short Description

Car wash bookings, wash history, staff and daily totals.

## Full Description

WashDesk helps car wash teams replace notebooks, loose slips and end-of-day
guesswork with one clean workspace.

Record walk-ins, manage bookings, track who washed each car, view wash history
by day, week or month, and keep daily totals ready without paperwork.

Key features:

- Record every wash with car, number plate, service and employee
- View bookings for the day
- See wash history by day, week and month
- Manage services and prices
- Keep team records
- Track daily, weekly and monthly totals
- Export offline backups
- Back up business data to cloud storage when configured

WashDesk is offline-first, so the wash bay can keep working even when the
connection is unreliable.

New manager accounts include a 5-day free trial. Continued access requires a
monthly subscription, currently R499.99/month unless Google Play shows a
different local price at checkout.
