## Description

WashDesk helps car wash teams run the day without notebooks, loose slips or
end-of-day guesswork.

Record walk-ins, manage bookings, track who washed each car, view wash history
by day, week or month, and keep daily totals ready from one simple workspace.

Built for busy wash bays, WashDesk keeps the core work clear:

- Capture each wash with car, number plate, service and employee
- See bookings due today
- Track completed washes in a clean history view
- Manage services and pricing
- Keep employee records
- View daily, weekly and monthly totals
- Export offline backups
- Back up business data to cloud storage when configured

WashDesk is offline-first, so the team can keep recording washes even when the
connection is unreliable.

New manager accounts include a 5-day free trial. Continued access requires a
monthly subscription, currently R499.99/month unless the App Store shows a
different local price at checkout.

## Review Notes

WashDesk is a car wash management app. Use the sign-up flow to create a manager
account and start the 5-day trial. The monthly subscription product ID is
`washdesk_monthly`.

The subscription screen shows a required-information block before purchase with
subscription title `WashDesk Monthly Subscription`, length `1 month`, the
auto-renewal wording, included service, price per 1-month subscription period,
Terms of Use link and Privacy Policy link.

Account deletion is available from Settings > Account > Delete account. The
flow asks for confirmation, requires typing the signed-in email address, deletes
the cloud account/backups when configured, removes the local WashDesk database
from the device, and returns to onboarding.

Cloud backup requires Supabase Storage configuration and the
`delete_current_user` RPC from `supabase/account_deletion.sql`.
