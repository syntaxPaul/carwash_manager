# WashDesk Screenshot Plan

Create separate screenshot sets for iPhone and Android phone.

## Required Screens

1. Landing page
   - Message: "Less paperwork. More paid washes."
   - Show the real WashDesk branding and hero image.

2. Dashboard
   - Show income/totals and the redesigned quick actions.

3. Record a wash / walk-in booking
   - Show fields for car, plate, service and employee.

4. Bookings
   - Show today's booking queue.

5. Wash history
   - Show day/week/month view, totals and completed wash details.

6. Reports
   - Show daily/monthly totals.

7. Settings / Cloud backup
   - Show cloud backup and offline export controls.

## Copy Overlays

Use short overlay captions only:

- "No more paper slips"
- "Record each wash in seconds"
- "Know who washed every car"
- "Daily totals without guesswork"
- "Backups for business records"

## Style Rules

- Use real in-app screens only.
- Do not show fake App Store badges inside screenshots.
- Do not show private customer information.
- Use clean demo data: common car models, sample plates and employee first
  names.
- Keep the app name visible in at least one screenshot.
