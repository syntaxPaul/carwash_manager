Delete your WashDesk account

WashDesk · account & data deletion

This page explains how to request deletion of your WashDesk manager account and the data associated with it.

Delete your account in the app

  1. Open the WashDesk app and sign in.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Tap Delete account.
  4. Read the confirmation notice and confirm. Deletion starts immediately.

Can't access the app?

Email support@roim4ads.com from the email address linked to your WashDesk account with the subject "Delete my WashDesk account". We verify the request and complete deletion within 30 days.

What is deleted

When your account is deleted, we remove: your account credentials and profile, your business details, and your cloud backups — including bookings, wash history, customer vehicle details and number plates, employee records, services, expenses and reports stored in WashDesk cloud storage. Your subscription entitlement records held by WashDesk are also removed.

What is kept, and for how long

Data on your device: WashDesk is offline-first, so records stored locally on your phone are under your control; they are removed when you delete the app or clear its data.

Billing records: subscription and payment history is held by Google Play or the Apple App Store under their own policies; WashDesk does not store your card details and cannot delete app store billing records.

Legal retention: where the law requires it (for example tax or fraud-prevention obligations), minimal transaction records may be retained for the legally required period and are then deleted.

Cancelling your subscription is separate from deleting your account: manage the subscription in Google Play or the App Store to stop future billing.
